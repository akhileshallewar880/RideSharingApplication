# Figma AI Prompt: VanYatra - Rural Ride Booking App Redesign

## **Role & Objective**
Act as a world-class UI/UX designer specializing in transportation and consumer apps (like Uber, Ola, BlaBlaCar). Your goal is to redesign the home screen of "VanYatra," a rural ride-booking service, to look premium, trustworthy, and highly engaging for a rural/semi-urban Indian audience. The design must be modern, accessible, and market-ready.

## **Design Style & Aesthetic**
- **Vibe:** Reliable, fast, friendly, and grounded.
- **Color Palette:**
  - **Primary:** Deep Emerald Green (Trust, Nature, Rural connection).
  - **Secondary:** Golden Yellow / Amber (Energy, Optimism, Call-to-Action).
  - **Background:** Clean White & Soft Light Greys for hierarchy.
  - **Accents:** Soft shadows for depth, subtle gradients for premium feel.
- **Typography:** Modern Sans-Serif (e.g., Poppins or Inter) – highly readable, with bold headings and clear labels.
- **Iconography:** Rounded, filled icons with soft edges.

## **Screen Layout & Key Components**

### **1. Header & Status Bar**
- **Background:** Rich gradient green curve blending seamlessly into the white body.
- **Elements:**
  - "VanYatra" logo in white with a subtle drop shadow.
  - Profile avatar (top right) with a status indicator (notification dot).
  - Hamburger menu (top left) for easy navigation.

### **2. Booking Card (Hero Section)**
- **Appearance:** A floating "Glassmorphism" card effect or a clean white card with high-elevation shadow, placed over the green header curve overlap.
- **Input Fields:**
  - **From / To:** Connected by a vertical dashed line journey visual. Input fields should have soft grey backgrounds with bold location text.
  - **Swap Button:** Circular animated button between fields.
- **Date Selector:** Two large, distinct toggle capsules: "Today, [Date]" (Active/Yellow) vs. "Tomorrow" (Inactive/Light Grey).
- **CTA Button:** Full-width "Search Rides" button in vibrant Golden Yellow with bold Black text. Add a subtle shimmer animation effect to suggest interactivity.

### **3. Animated Marketing Banners (The "Wow" Factor)**
- **Section Title:** "Offers & Deals" (Icon: Price Tag).
- **Carousel:** Horizontal scrollable cards.
- **Banner Design:**
  - **Card 1 (First Ride Free):** Gradient Peach/Orange background. Isolate 3D illustration of a festive party popper or a gift box. Text: "Get your 1st ride absolutely FREE!" with checkmarks: "No hidden charges".
  - **Card 2 (Referral):** Blue/Teal gradient. 3D illustration of two friends high-fiving. Text: "Refer & Earn ₹50".
  - **Animation:** Suggest subtle continuous movement (e.g., floating elements) in the banner illustrations.

### **4. Service Categories (Quick Access)**
- A row of circular icons with labels below the booking card for quick filters: "Auto", "Bike", "Car", "Inter-city".
- Active state should highlight the icon in Green.

### **5. Bottom Navigation Bar**
- **Style:** Clean white bar with top border shadow.
- **Icons:** Home (Active Green), Services, My Rides (Ticket icon), Account (User icon).
- **Active State:** Filled icon + Label.
- **Inactive State:** Outline icon + Label (Grey).

## **Micro-Interactions & details to include**
- **Feedback:** Ripple effects on buttons.
- **Loading:** Skeleton screens for data fetching.
- **Accessibility:** High contrast text, large touch targets (min 44px).
- **Visual Polish:**
  - Use 3D clay-style illustrations for empty states or banners to look trendy.
  - Smooth rounded corners (16px - 24px) for cards.

## **Output Request**
Generate a high-fidelity mobile UI mockup for the "Home Screen". Show the "Search Card" interacting (e.g., Dropdown for location suggestions). Provide a variant showing the "Offers Banner" in focus.
